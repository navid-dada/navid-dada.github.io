Mealtime flexibility

NOVOMIX®30 allows flexible injection timing 8-13
  • BHI needs to be administrated at least 30 minutes before a meal8
  • Adherence to BHI injection timing is poor8
  • NOVOMIX® 30 can be taken just before or immediately after a meal8 8-13

It’s easy to upgrade your patients to NovoMix®30 from BHI by using a simple 1:1 conversion.13 Start NovoMix® 30 with the same total daily insulin dose as their BHI regimen.*13

The dose can be administered up to three times daily.14,15
